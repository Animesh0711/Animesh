Automated Plant Watering System

An automated system for watering plants based on soil moisture levels, using sensors and Raspberry Pi to ensure optimal plant care.

▼
Description:

The Automated Plant Watering System is an innovative project designed to ensure that plants receive the right amount of water at the right time. Using a combination of soil moisture sensors and a Raspberry Pi, this system monitors the moisture level of the soil and activates a water pump when needed.

The system is equipped with a soil moisture sensor that continuously measures the soil's moisture content. The data collected by the sensor is sent to the Raspberry Pi, which then decides when to activate the water pump based on pre-set thresholds. This ensures that the plants are watered efficiently, conserving water and reducing the need for manual intervention.

Additional features include a web interface for monitoring and adjusting the system settings remotely, making it a highly versatile solution for plant care.

Star Trail Photography

A project involving capturing long-exposure photographs of star trails using a Raspberry Pi camera.

▼
Description:

The Star Trail Photography project aims to capture the beautiful and mesmerizing trails of stars over long periods. Using a Raspberry Pi camera, this project involves taking a series of long-exposure photos at regular intervals throughout the night.

The captured images are then combined using software to create a single photograph that shows the trails of stars as they move across the night sky. This project requires careful planning of the camera setup, exposure times, and post-processing techniques to achieve the desired effect.

It offers a great opportunity to explore astrophotography and learn about the effects of long exposures and star movements.
